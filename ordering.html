Ordering With Us:

Please include the following in your email when placing an order:

- Rosette name and code

- Quantity of rosettes. This is the quantity of PACKS, not individual rosettes.

- Whether pins, hooks or sticky tabs are required.

- Date required

- Delivery Address

- Method of shipping
